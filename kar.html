Karthik's Lemon Rice Recipe

Ingridients required

Primary Ingridients

  • 250 grams of rice
  • Handfull of ground nuts
  • corriander leaves
  • Turmeric
  • Salt as Required

For garnishing

  • One tablespoon oil
  • Elachi
  • Moong dal

Procedure to cook Lemon rice

  1. Step 1 :- First we take 250 grams of rice and soak them well in water for about 2 hours.
  2. Step 2:- After soaking them wash them throughly.
  3. Step 3:- After washing them place it an rice cooker to cook the rice properly.
  4. Step 4:- Now for a glass of rice pour two glasses of water into the cooker and switch on the cooker.
  5. Step 5:- Now after 4 to 5 whistles stop the cooker and wait to let it cool down.
  6. Step 6:- After step 5 now we should add tuermic based on our taste and mix it throughly.
  7. step 7:- Now place small pan and add one table spoon if oil into it and heat it for 5 minutes and add a hand full of groundnuts into the pan and add required garments into it.
  8. Now mix the things in the pan to the rice and add some mirchi based on your taste and add some salt based on your taste.
  9. Mix the materials througly and serve it into plates it tastes delicious.
